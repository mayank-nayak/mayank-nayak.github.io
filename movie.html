My Favorite Movie

Rush Hour

Why is Rush Hour my favorite movie?

Rush Hour is my favorite movie because it makes feel extremely nostalgic about my childhood. Every single time I watch it, I feel like I am watching it for the first time. The acting, jokes, and narrative never get old. Moreover, the combination of Jackie Chan and Chris Tucker is nothing less than perfect. For all the reasons above and more, Rush Hour will probably always be my favorite movie.

What is the movie about?

When the eleven-year-old daughter of the Chinese Consul in Los Angeles, Han, is kidnapped in broad daylight, the desperate father insists in summoning his old friend and Hong Kong Police ace detective, Inspector Lee, to assist the FBI in the investigation. However, as the bureau is far from keen on allowing the newcomer to get in their way, they pair him up with the motor-mouthed LAPD Detective, James Carter, to watch him over. Of course, none of them is particularly happy about this awkward partnership; nevertheless, the mismatched duo of reluctant partners must team up to solve this mystery before the FBI.
